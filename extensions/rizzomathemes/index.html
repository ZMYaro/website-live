Themes for Rizzoma

Rizzoma is a highly capable and flexible application, but its interface can also be complicated and unintuitive. These extensions change the appearance to look slightly more familiar and attempt to slightly simplify things.

Material Design for Rizzoma

2016-2019 Get for Chrome Get for Firefox Get for Opera
Source Code

The most extensive of my Rizzoma themes, Material Design for Rizzoma brings some ideas from my Rizzoma for Android mock-ups to the desktop web app. It cleans up some of the layout, simplifies the color palette and layers, and does its best to implement aspects of the Material guidelines for things such as avatars, shadows, and icons.
