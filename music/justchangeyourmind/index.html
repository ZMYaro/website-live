Just Change Your Mind

2019

“Just Be” × “Change Your Mind” music video

This idea came to me while I was watching the Steven Universe season 5 finale and feeling it had strong Kinky Boots vibes (especially “you change the world when you change your mind”), and no one had done it yet, so I made it for Pride month 2019.

Video: Steven Universe S5E32 “Change Your Mind, Part 4”, directed by Joe Johnston, Kat Morris, and Liz Artinian
Music: “Raise You Up / Just Be” from Kinky Boots, music and lyrics by Cyndi Lauper
Editing: Zachary Yaro
Created with Adobe Premiere CS6

This is a fan-made (not for profit) video, and the materials in this video are not related in any way to myself or each other outside of it. Please support the original works.
