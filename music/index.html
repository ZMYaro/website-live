Music

  • Just Change Your Mind

    “Just Be” × “Change Your Mind” music video

This section has been largely abandoned since 2012; your mileage may vary.

I am not a great composer (although I have tried), but I do enjoy singing and writing lyrics to existing tunes. If you see me typing on my phone for a while, I may be working on one of these...or just programming.

Also note that for the most part, each of my parodies is intended for a certain audience.

  • “Mr. Dark Side”

    Based on “Mr. Brightside”

    A song about the Skywalkers. Originally just two amusing lines, somehow turned into this, and finally finished for the premiere of Episode VII.

  • “An Awful Lot Of Moffat”

    Based on “An Awful Lot Of Running”

    A song about Steven Moffat's contributions to Doctor Who, written to celebrate the premiere of Doctor Who series 7.

  • “Bits Inside My Head”

    Based on “Pictures In My Head”

    A response to the announcement of Google Wave's death date. Based on a song from The Muppets. The dates happened to be announced around the time I saw the movie and was listening to the soundtrack.

  • “Back To TJ” (preview)

    Based on “Get Back To Hogwarts”

    Intended for TJ people (or anyone returning to school at the end of the summer). It helps to have seen A Very Potter Musical

  • “Google Wave Ain't Goin' Down”

    Based on “Voldemort Is Goin' Down”

    Part of my effort to save Google Wave. Based on a song from A Very Potter Musical

  • “To Rodef” or “TRSTY is Totally Awesome”

    Based on “Get Back To Hogwarts”

    Written for friends in TRSTY. It helps to have seen A Very Potter Musical.

  • “Bring The Syslab Back To Me”

    Based on “Bring That Magic Back To Me”

    Only people who go to TJ and were at Harlam second session 2009 will completely get it, but any geek should enjoy it.

  • “Still A Spy”

    Based on “Still Alive”

    Intended for players of Team Fortress and Portal.

  • “Still Alive - TJ Version”

    Based on “Still Alive”

    Intended for TJ students or people who know about TJHSST. Fun fact: A good portion of this was created during gym class last school year (2008-2009).

  • “Only At TJ”

    Based on “I Want It That Way”

    One of my favorites of all my parodies. Particularly funny for those who go to or know about TJHSST. Fun fact: this parody was much more inspired by “Weird Al” Yankovic's “eBay”, so we technically parodied a parody.
