F.A.Q.

In this case “frequently” means “asked enough times that I put it here so you can stop asking.” that does not mean I hate all of these questions, it just means that there is now a way to get your answers without asking me in person. Anyway, here we go.


Q: What is your favorite programming language?

A: It really depends on the project. I generally like using HTML5/CSS3/JavaScript, but that is not optimal for GUI-less programs and code that needs access to things on the computer. For quick little programs I tend to use Python. For larger things I will probably use Java. For things that require fancy graphics I will probably use Flash (with ActionScript 3).


Q: What do you mean when you call yourself a “manual blogger”?

A: I mean that my blog is (was) entirely manual. I added each post to the news page, my home page, the RSS feed manually. In a recent update I made a small change so that all news posts are contained in a single text file that is converted into HTML with JavaScript. This makes it a bit easier to add (and view) posts while still giving me 100% control. I will admit I have moved away slightly from being completely manual with the wave versions of my blog posts, but wave is worth it.


Q: Why are your answers so long?

A: Yeah, I do that too much. I apologize. I will try to keep them shorter.


Q: Will you fix my computer/printer/phone/...?

A: Maybe, if you agree to not blame me for anything that goes wrong. Likely no.


Q: Will you write/design a website/program for me?

A: Will you provide an incentive?


Q: Why do you dislike Facebook?

A: It makes it very easy to join and import information, but much more difficult to get any of your data back out. I also dislike a product being based on the idea that you should do something just because 100 other people did.


Q: What do you mean when you say FB is mind control?

A: If an admin sent a message to every FB user saying the best video of someone standing on xer head wins 500 “Facebook points,” a lot of people would stand on their heads.


Q: Why do you dislike emotions?

A: Uncontrolled emotion is the cause of many stupid things.


Q: Why do you dislike religion?

A: I dislike most religions (particularly theistic) because, they cause people to deny logic in favor of “faith” and because they are used as excuses for many terrible acts. I think things like building communities, helping people, and morals can exist without deities.


Q: How can you be Jewish and agnostic?

A: I am Jewish in the sense that I follow the culture without most of the religion. I do not see the Bible as non-fiction.


Q: You are going to hell for that!

A: Mmmm...toasty! But seriously, if you consider a Dante-esque hell logically, it does not seem like it would be that terrible. The worse torture would likely be boredom. Also, a lot of awesome people will be there.


Q: Why do you hate so many things?

A: Everyone hates things. They just hate some things less, and they are better than me at keeping their mouths shut.


Q: What are those four-digit codes you use?

A: They are created using a system I made to turn almost any word into a four-digit code. I usually use them as shorthand ways to refer to projects or entities.
