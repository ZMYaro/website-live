Why are images useful/important?

Images are (in case you did not know) pictures. Images can allow you to (as well as showing plain old images) show preformatted text or text with special formatting, unusual fonts that the average user may not have, animations, and possibly, image maps (but that is another page).


How to ADD an image

To add an image to you webpage, you add the <img> tag to your HTML.
