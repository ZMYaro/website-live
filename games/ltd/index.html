Legitimate Tower Defense

Play LTD

Legitimate Tower Defense was the first game I worked on with the group that would come to call itself Inverted Productions. I primarily worked on graphics for LTD, though I also had a small part in designing some aspects of gameplay. While working on LTD, I gained a lot more skill converting bitmap images to vector graphics, and I also gained my reputation for drawing at an average of 1000% zoom.

The game's core concept is literally defending towers from increasingly-ridiculous falling objects—from wrecking balls to vehicles to office supplies.
