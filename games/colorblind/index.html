Color Blind

2016 Play Color Blind

Developed with Robert Pruden and Tiffany McFarlane

Awarded Best Design at BrickHack II

Dr. Chroma's nemesis has broken into his lab and destroyed his Chroma Scanner, damaging the Chroma Force and wreaking havoc on the visible light spectrum. Dr. Chroma's only hope is to attempt to find the damaged pieces of the Chroma Scanner scattered throughout his lab, rebuild the Chroma Scanner, and repair the Chroma Force.
