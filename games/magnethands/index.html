Dr. Magnethands

Play Dr. Magnethands
Source Code

Dr. Magnethands is a tabletop RPG for three or more drunk people, created by Grant Howitt. My friends and I enjoy playing Dr. Magnethands, but we frequently forget to bring pencils and paper when drinking together, so with his permission, I ported Grant's original tabletop rules to a mobile web app.

Dr. Magnethands provides the essence of a tabletop RPG in a quick, simple, and silly format perfect for people new to the genre, not looking for something too serious, or too impaired for math and dice-rolling.
