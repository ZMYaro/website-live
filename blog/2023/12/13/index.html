I Fixed Highlighting Text In Trello

2023-12-13

A while back, Trello inexplicably decided to make text selection highlights invisible. I finally got frustrated enough by that to make a browser extension that fixes it. This is probably the most basic extension I have ever released, and one that shouldn't have ever been needed, but here we are.
