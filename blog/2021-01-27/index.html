Rewind Theater Update

2021-01-27

Rewind Theater is a simple app based on the old IGN series of the same name, in which they scrubbed through trailers and highlighted details likely to be missed on a first viewing. I threw the initial app together in under 48 hours before an event where we did a live analysis of the Doctor Who 50th anniversary trailers in 2013, but this month, I rolled out a variety of features and improvements that did not make it in by that deadline, including a nicer toolbar, new controls, better video loading times, offline functionality, and more. ➡https://rewindtheater.zmyaro.com

Also posted to Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter.
