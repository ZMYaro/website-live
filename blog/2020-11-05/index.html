PortalZ: The 2020 Slice

2020-11-05

In the middle of summer lockdown, I had a sudden desire to revisit PortalZ. I may not have had time to build every planned test chamber or correct every questionable 2009 design decision in the revamped chambers, but today, I am excited to release PortalZ: The 2020 Slice, a pack of 14 test chambers for Portal 1! Please feel free to give it a shot, share if you like it, and let me know what you think!
