Dr. Magnethands Is Back!

2020-12-23

Whether you are (hopefully safely) visiting family for Christmas, or staying in lockdown with flatmates, here's a new game you can play: Dr. Magnethands, the tabletop RPG for three or more drunk people, now available as a convenient phone app!

If you're saying, “Didn't you already make that in 2016?” yes, but then Google killed a service that app needed to run. Now, after a few years' hiatus, Dr. Magnethands is back with some new (Google-free) stuff under the hood, a couple new tricks (dark theme is go!), and ready for you to once again enjoy the simplest, least serious TTRPG you've ever played (alcohol optional). Try it now at magnethands.fun.
