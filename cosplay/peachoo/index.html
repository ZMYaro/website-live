Peachoo (a.k.a. Boo!Peach, a.k.a. Peach-a-Boo, a.k.a. PeachyBoo a.k.a. Booette, a.k.a. Boosette)

From the New Super Mario Bros. U Deluxe Super Crown meme.

Worn at RIT Cosplay Spookfest 2018. Also worn for Halloween 2018 and Halloween 2019.

This section is still under construction.

Made for Cosplay Spookfest 2018 soon after the New Super Mario Bros. U Deluxe Super Crown meme took off. Based the design on art by Michael Lee Lunsford.

Also wore for Halloween that year, and brought back for Halloween 2019.

Crown went through a few paper prototypes before making the real thing out of craft foam. Balls on the crown were wooden beads, secured in place with toothpicks and hot glue. Mushroom was pink fabric with spots made from white felt, stuffed, and put over my Zero II hat. Brooch and earrings were hand-sculpted out of oven bake clay. Collar pins were laser cut out of cardboard and then painted.
