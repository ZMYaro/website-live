The Scout

From Team Fortress 2.

Worn at the World Video Game Hall Of Fame 2019 induction celebration and RetroGameCon 7 (2019).

This section is still under construction.

Originally a closet cosplay (except the bat and headset were not technically stored in my closet).

Then, in 2019, I remembered I did this cosplay and redid it with a more accurate shirt and hat. Made a new headset out of a couple pairs of broken headphones (the mic is actually a piece of one's headband, heat gunned into shape, with pipe cleaner fuzz glued to it 😆).

Also got a Sandman-like prop bat and shaved better before wearing the remade costume out.
