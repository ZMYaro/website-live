Captain Dorito

From the Internet meme based on Captain America.

Worn at Tora-Con 2018.

This section is still under construction.

I had started a streak of cosplaying triangles to Tora-Con. For 2018, as a joke, I was Captain Dorito, as a reference to the meme that Captain America is shaped like a Dorito.

The costume was a super cheap Captain America shirt and mask (ground a bit off the mask with a rotary tool to fit my nose), a spare piece of card board I spray painted, and a bag of Doritos acquired specifically for this purpose.
