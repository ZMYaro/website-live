Final John

From The Adventure Zone: Balance.

Worn at RIT Cosplay Spook Bash 2019.

This section is still under construction.

Wings made from PVC after several design iterations. Originally meant as a prototype of wings for a Snow Fest 2020 costume, but decided would work well as a Halloween outfit, so finished them (Snow Fest version would have used translucent pipes with lights inside, and a better structure for holding the orbs; I might still make it some day).

For hair and make-up, tried to incorporate the rainbow glow and years of damage, with an irridescent effect with a combination of hair wax and other products, and a colored shades of eyeshadow along contour, and then cracks drawn on top of everything else.
