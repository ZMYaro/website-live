Spinel (24-Hour Version)

From Steven Universe: The Movie.

This section is still under construction.

TL;DR: Made in about 12 hours of construction (over a 24-hour period), after about 12 hours of planning. Reused old dress from earlier iteration of Umbridge, tights from Peachoo, Women's March hat, cane from Zero II, and magnets from Tony, plus white spray paint, gorilla tape, spare cardboard, and wire left over from other projects. Only items purchased were the shirt (thrift store) and pink spray paint.
