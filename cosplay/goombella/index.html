Goombella

From Paper Mario: The Thousand Year Door.

Worn at RIT Duxpo 2018.

This section is still under construction.

For Duxpo, the fake con held while much of the Rochester/RIT cosplay community was at Katsucon 2018, we decided to throw together last-minute low-budget Paper Mario cosplays. The shirt, tie, and wig I already had; the hat was a friend's; the head lamp was the screw cap of a container; the Tattle Log was a lucky thrift store find; and the fang was a random torn piece of white cardboard.
